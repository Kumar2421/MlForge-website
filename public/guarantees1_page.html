Technical definitions for system-level operational ownership. ML Forge provides high-fidelity evidence, enabling engineering teams to reason about risk and reproducibility before deployment.

Contractual Architecture

System Guarantees.

Infrastructure integrity ensured
lan KERNEL_ISOLATION

Local Runtime Integrity

Training, evaluation, and export run exclusively on your infrastructure. ML Forge never requires high-side dataset or model artifact transmission to third-party services.

history BIT_PARITY

Predictable Execution

Identical inputs (dataset version, configuration, runtime environment) produce repeatable, inspectable outputs. Every parameter hash is preserved for exact state reconstruction.

account_tree LINEAGE_PROTOCOL

Traceability Chain

An unbroken lineage chain connects dataset versions to run configurations, execution outputs, and final evaluation results. Every deployed artifact is attributable to its originating evidence.

inventory_2 AUDIT_RETENTION

Permanent Metadata Audit

Config and run metadata for every experiment is stored to facilitate re-runs or forensic audits. Parameterization and artifact sets are immutable objects in our distribution hub.

verified HASH_LOCK

Verified Export Bundles

Model exports are cryptographically packaged with associated evidence (metrics, configs, run IDs). Verification of training provenance is baked into every binary.

terminal STEP_VERBOSE

Explicit Workflow Handlers

Actions are executed as explicit sequential steps, never as silent black-box background decisions. Inputs and outcomes are exposed via real-time telemetry streams.

Infrastructure Responsibility Matrix

System Boundaries.

warning

Model Performance Targets

ML Forge cannot guarantee accuracy, robustness, or fairness targets. Outcomes are strictly dependent on your data quality, labeling policy, and evaluation design.

memory

Cross-Hardware Reproducibility

Identical results are not guaranteed across materially different hardware, CUDA toolchain versions, or nondeterministic third-party libraries. Reproducibility is hardware-locked to your runtime environment.

spellcheck

Annotation Authenticity

ML Forge provides the workflow structure and evidence capture, but does not guarantee that your annotations are accurate, consistent, or complete. Data truth remains a human-in-the-loop responsibility.

gavel

Governance & Compliance

Platform auditability supports your governance program but does not replace it. Teams must maintain their own access controls, approval workflows, and validation protocols.

science

Engineering Design Integrity

The system makes evidence reviewable but does not prevent poor experimental design, data leakage, or inappropriate metric choice. Accountably remains with the engineering lead.

speed

Inference Performance

Latency, throughput, and memory footprint in downstream environments are not guaranteed by the Forge bin. These must be validated against your target deployment constraints.
